STEPS

1). Open your snowflake account
2). On bottom left click on your account name
3). Then again click on your account name
4). Click view account details
5). Copy value of account identifier
6). Go to power bi and use snowflake connector
7). Paste account identifier value in Server Section
8). For Warehouse name go to Admin Section in snowflake
9). Then go to Warehouses
10). Copy your warehouse name and paste it into Warehouse Section in Power bi
11). Click connect
12). Enter your snowflake account name and password
13). click sign in
14). Select your tables to be loaded to power bi 
15). Transform data OR load data
